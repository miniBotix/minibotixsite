About miniBotix

miniBotix is a universal learning platform for students in all streams — including engineering, design, arts, and more. We provide high-quality courses with an interactive learning experience.

Students can explore a wide range of subjects, engage with real-time examples, take topic-based online tests, and complete hands-on tasks. Every course is structured for in-depth understanding and practical knowledge.

On course completion, students receive performance-based certifications. Our platform simulates real company interview rounds and industry projects — helping learners gain skills to meet global standards.

Whether you’re a beginner or advanced learner, miniBotix empowers you to grow into an innovator through interactive sessions, practice tests, and step-by-step challenges.
