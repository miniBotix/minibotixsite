PCB Designing Course

Master the art of designing printed circuit boards from scratch

🔍 Course Overview

Dive into the fascinating world of electronics with our hands-on PCB Designing Course. Whether you're an electronics enthusiast, student, or hardware developer, this course equips you with the skills to design, simulate, and create professional-quality PCBs.

📚 What You'll Learn

  • Basics of circuit design and schematic creation
  • Using PCB design software like EasyEDA, KiCad or Altium (visual demos)
  • Component placement and track routing techniques
  • Design rules, layers, and ground planes
  • Generating Gerber files for manufacturing
  • Importing custom footprints and libraries
  • Tips for signal integrity and power efficiency

🛠️ Real-Time Projects

  • Single-layer LED Blinker Circuit
  • Multi-layer Temperature Sensor Module
  • Custom Arduino Nano-Compatible Board

🏆 Certification

After completing the design tasks and final quiz, you'll receive a certified badge as a "PCB Design Specialist" to showcase your skills.

🎯 Who This Course is For

  • Engineering students & electronics hobbyists
  • Professionals looking to prototype hardware
  • Anyone interested in circuit board design
